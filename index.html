Pikko Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 13, 2026

Pikko is a private, invite-only photo-conversation app for families and small groups. This policy explains what information Pikko collects, why, and the choices you have. Pikko is currently offered as a closed beta.

In this policy, “Pikko,” “we,” or “us” refers to the developer who operates the app. If you have any questions, contact us at dmw7752@gmail.com.

Information we collect

Account and profile

  • Phone number — used to sign you in and verify your identity. Verification is handled by Google Firebase Authentication.
  • Display name and username — how you appear to others in the app.
  • Profile photo and bio — optional information you choose to add.

Content you create

  • Photos you capture in the app or select from your device to post.
  • Text and interactions — captions, notes, reactions, replies, and the groups you create or join.

We access your camera and photo library only when you take or choose a photo to share. Pikko does not scan or upload your photo library in the background.

Device and technical information

  • Push notification token — so we can deliver notifications to your device.
  • Device model, operating system, and app version — to keep the app working correctly and to diagnose problems.
  • Time zone — to schedule time-based features (like daily prints) in your local time.

Feedback

If you send feedback through the app, we receive the message you write along with basic app and device details to help us reproduce the issue.

What we do not collect

Pikko does not collect your precise location or your contacts, and it contains no advertising, no advertising identifiers, and no third-party analytics or tracking.

How we use information

  • To sign you in and keep your account secure.
  • To show your posts to the groups and people you choose to share them with.
  • To deliver notifications you've enabled.
  • To run time-based features in your local time zone.
  • To operate, maintain, and improve the app, and to respond to your feedback.

How information is shared

With other people in the app

Content you post is visible to the groups and connections you share it with. Your display name, username, profile photo, and bio are visible to people you're connected with in Pikko.

With service providers

  • Google Firebase (Authentication, Cloud Firestore, Cloud Storage, Cloud Functions, and Cloud Messaging) stores and processes your account, content, and notification data on our behalf. See Firebase’s privacy information.
  • GIF search provider (Klipy). When you search for a GIF, your search terms are sent to our GIF provider to return results. Your Pikko account information is not shared with them.
  • Development tracker (GitHub). Feedback you submit may be recorded as a private issue for us to track and fix.

What we never do

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not share it for advertising. We may disclose information if required by law or to protect the safety of our users.

Storage and security

Your data is stored on Google Firebase infrastructure. Access is restricted by security rules, and data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS. No method of storage or transmission is perfectly secure, but we take reasonable measures to protect your information.

Data retention and deletion

We keep your information while your account is active. You can ask us to delete your account and its associated content at any time by emailing dmw7752@gmail.com. We will delete it within a reasonable period, except where we're required to retain certain information by law.

Your choices

  • Update your profile information at any time in the app.
  • Control or turn off notifications in your device settings.
  • Request access to, or deletion of, your data by emailing us.

Children

Pikko is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we will delete it.

International users

Pikko is operated using service providers based in the United States, and your information may be stored and processed there.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we'll revise the “Last updated” date above.

Contact

Questions about this policy or your data? Email dmw7752@gmail.com.
